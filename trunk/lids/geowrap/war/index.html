GeoNames LIDS

Welcome to the Linked Data Service (LIDS) for GeoNames. Currently we support the findNearby and findNearbyPlaceName services.

Input: lat and lng. Output: RDF with the nearest place. (LIDS description).

E.g. /findNearby?lat=49.009&lng=8.412#point. E.g. /findNearbyPlaceName?lat=49.009&lng=8.412#point.
