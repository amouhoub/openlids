GeoNames LIDS

Welcome to the geo-location Linked Data Service (LIDS). The service wraps selected GeoNames and Google services for use with Linked Data.

Currently we support the following services:

findNearby
Input: lat and lng. Output: RDF with the nearest place. E.g. /findNearby?lat=49.009&lng=8.412#point.
findNearbyPlaceName
Input: lat and lng. Output: RDF with the nearest place. E.g. /findNearbyPlaceName?lat=49.009&lng=8.412#point.
findNearbyWikipedia
Input: lat and lng. Output: RDF with the nearest place from Wikipedia (DBpedia in our case). E.g. /findNearbyWikipedia?lat=49.009&lng=8.412#point.
geocode
Input: street address. Output: RDF with lat/lng. E.g. /geocode?address=Englerstr.11,Karlsruhe,Germany#point.
